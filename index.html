About

Designer who speaks developer

I design tools that keep complex operations running — from logistics platforms connecting hundreds of drivers, to mobile apps on factory floors, to vessel-tracking dashboards at Indonesia's largest cement group. I turn messy, paper-heavy workflows into digital products teams actually want to use.

What sets me apart: I understand both sides. I can sketch wireframes in the morning and debug a Flutter build in the afternoon. This means fewer handoff gaps, faster iterations, and designs that are actually feasible to ship.

My process starts with the people — shadowing operators on factory floors, interviewing port managers, mapping pain points. Then I prototype fast, test with real users, and iterate until it works. No ego, just results.
