About

Designer who speaks developer.

I design tools that keep complex operations running — logistics platforms connecting hundreds of drivers, mobile apps on factory floors, vessel-tracking dashboards at Indonesia's largest cement group. I turn messy paper workflows into digital products teams actually want to use.

What sets me apart: I understand both sides. I can sketch wireframes in the morning and debug a Flutter build in the afternoon. Fewer handoff gaps, faster iterations, designs that actually ship.

"My process starts with the people — shadowing operators on factory floors, interviewing port managers. Then I prototype fast, test with real users, and iterate until it works. No ego, just results."
