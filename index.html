About

Designer by choice, strategist by instinct

I design tools that keep complex operations running. From logistics platforms connecting drivers, shippers, and transporters — to mobile apps that catch factory breakdowns before they happen — to vessel-tracking systems that ease port congestion. Messy data, manual forms, coordination chaos — I turn them into clear, trustworthy tools teams enjoy using.

"I approach design like a ranked match — strategic, fast, and adaptive. Years of playing Dota 2 and Mobile Legends taught me to read patterns, stay calm in chaos, and always find the next move."

I'm not a coding wizard or math genius — so I chose to master what bridges it all: digital product design. I thrive on understanding people, digging for insights, and turning messy problems into clear, useful solutions.
